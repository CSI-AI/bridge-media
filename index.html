Bridge AI Office Publisher

First-party publishing tool for the Bridge AI Office channel — @bridgeaioffice

Bridge AI Office Publisher is a private, single-operator tool that publishes our own original short-form videos to our own social media accounts. It is operated by Bridge AI Office (브릿지 AI 운영실), a small-business content operation based in Busan, South Korea. It is not a multi-tenant service and does not act on behalf of any third-party users.

What it does

  • Authenticates our own TikTok account (@bridgeaioffice) using TikTok Login Kit.
  • Uploads and publishes videos we created ourselves to that same account using the Content Posting API (Direct Post) with the video.publish scope.
  • Before each publish, the operator reviews and explicitly approves the creator info, caption, privacy level, and comment/duet/stitch settings.

Content

Our videos are short educational clips (≤15s) about how a non-developer runs a small business with AI and automation. Characters and narration are AI-assisted and clearly disclosed. We do not post copyrighted third-party media, and we do not make exaggerated income or investment claims.

Single first-party account Own original content AI-assisted, disclosed No third-party user data

Scopes we request & why

  • user.info.basic / Login Kit — to sign in to and identify our own account for publishing.
  • video.publish (Content Posting API, Direct Post) — to publish our own finished videos directly to our own account without manual re-uploading.
